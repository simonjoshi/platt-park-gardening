A note from our owners

We started zeezoo in our garage on Tennyson Street.

We're Camila and Theo — born-and-raised Denverites, married in '17, parents to a very small human named Mateo. After Camila's mom got sick, we found ourselves doing laundry for half the neighborhood. Turns out we love it. So we bought a couple of commercial machines, partnered with our friends at Cottonwood Detergent Co., and started zeezoo washme out of pure stubborn affection for clean clothes.

Today we wash for ~400 Denver homes and a handful of Airbnbs & gyms across the Front Range. Every load is sorted by hand, washed cold, dried gentle, and folded the way our abuela taught us. Same hands, every week.

How it works

Four steps, zero quarters.

Schedule once and we'll be there every week (or every other) — same day, same window, same hands. Skip or pause anytime in two taps.

i.

Tell us when
