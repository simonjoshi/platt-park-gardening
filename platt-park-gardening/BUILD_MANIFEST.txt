PROJECT OVERVIEW
================
A production-ready, single-page MVP landing site for Platt Park Gardening 
Service built with Next.js, React, and Tailwind CSS.

Target Audience: Affluent Denver homeowners ($500K+ properties) in Platt Park 
and Cherry Creek areas.
